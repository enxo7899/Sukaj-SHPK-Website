# Logo Background Removal Instructions

## Quick Method (Online Tool)
1. Visit https://www.remove.bg/
2. Upload `/public/media/hero/logo.png`
3. Download the transparent PNG
4. Replace the original file

## Alternative Method (Photoshop/GIMP)
1. Open logo.png in Photoshop or GIMP
2. Use Magic Wand tool to select white background
3. Delete selection
4. Save as PNG with transparency

## Alternative Method (Preview on Mac)
1. Open logo.png in Preview
2. Click "Instant Alpha" tool
3. Click and drag on white background
4. Delete selection
5. Export as PNG

The logo will then display perfectly on dark backgrounds without the white box.
